Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Emergency Medical Form program, sponsored by Financial Health ("we," "us," or "our"), collects, uses, and protects the information you provide through this website.

The short version: We only collect your name and mailing address so we can send you a free emergency medical form and magnetic packet. We do not sell your information, and we use only essential cookies.

1. Information We Collect

When you request a free packet, we collect only the information necessary to fulfill your request:

  • Your first and last name
  • Your mailing address (street, city, state, and ZIP code)

We do not collect medical information through this website. Any medical details you write on the physical form stay with you and are never transmitted to us.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To prepare, package, and mail your free emergency medical form and magnetic packet.
  • To contact you about your order if there is a problem processing or shipping it.
  • To keep records required for the operation of this free program.

3. How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to safeguard the information you provide. Access is limited to those who need it to fulfill and ship your request.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your mailing address with shipping or fulfillment partners solely to deliver your packet, or where required by law.

5. Cookies

We use only essential cookies and local browser storage to remember your cookie preference and keep the site functioning. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies. You can manage your choice at any time using the "Cookie Preferences" link in the site footer.

6. Optional Donations

If you choose to make an optional donation, payment is processed by a third-party payment provider. We do not store your full payment card details. Please review that provider's privacy policy for how they handle payment information.

7. Your Choices

  • You may request that we correct or delete the personal information you provided.
  • You may decline non-essential cookies at any time.
  • Providing your information is voluntary, but we cannot ship a packet without a name and address.

8. Children's Privacy

This website is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Contact Financial Health at (586) 371-2312.
